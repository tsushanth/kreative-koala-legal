Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 14, 2025

Audexa, ShortMail, ScribeAI, Riddleverse, and all Kreative Koala LLC products ("we," "our," or "us") value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered applications developed by Kreative Koala LLC (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register for the Service, we collect your name, email address, and other account details.
  • Content: Any files, messages, or data you input or upload while using the Service.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Service, including access times, features used, and device information.
  • Device Information: Details such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar technologies to track user preferences and improve our Service.

c. Third-Party Information

We may receive information about you from third-party integrations or services you connect to our platform, such as email or calendar services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve the Service: Ensuring the functionality and performance of our app.
  • To Personalize User Experience: Customizing the features and recommendations offered to you.
  • To Communicate With You: Sending updates, support messages, and promotional content (with your consent).
  • To Maintain Security: Detecting and preventing unauthorized access, fraud, or abuse.
  • To Comply With Legal Obligations: Ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Important: AI and data processing used in our software is not used to develop, improve, or train generalized AI and/or ML models.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating our Service.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. After this period, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@kreativekoala.llc.

6. Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will promptly delete it.
